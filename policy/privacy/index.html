Privacy Policy


When you use our software’s and services, we collect some information from you to provide you the best of features we can. This is a big responsibility and we do understand this, also we do our best to protect your information, because we at ZEST believe that your privacy matters.


Information that we collect.

We collect information based on the software or service you use. All your information is saved on our server except some of your confidential information such as account details. ZEST never stores your account details.

You may access some of our services by signing up for a ZEST Account and providing us with some personal information (typically your name, email address, and a password). This account information is used to authenticate you when you access ZEST services and protect your account from unauthorized access by others. You can edit or delete your account at any time by contacting us.


Information that we share.

Information that we share depends on the software and services you are using keeping in mind that your data is safe. ZEST may share required/relevant information about you with advertisers, business partners, sponsors, and other third parties. However, we only divulge aggregate information about our users and will not share personally identifiable information with any third party without your express consent.


We reserve the right at our discretion to make changes to this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes.
